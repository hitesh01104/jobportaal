Position Details : NOC Engineer
Location: Mumbai,Maharastra
Openings: 1
Salary : 3.40 LPA
Description:
NOC Engineer Requirement
    Network Operation Centre (NOC) Engineer have Following main roles:
  • To ensure maximum possible service availability and performance.
  • Faults are resolved within SLA as agreed with customer.
  • To provision customer network services on Access/aggregation equipment based on MPLS TP.
  • To provide required support services to Field support teams.
  • Must be willing in working in shift duties.
  • Good communication skills.
    • Required Qualification and Experience:
  • Educational Qualification : Bachelor of Engineering (preferably in Electronics and communication).
  • Good Knowledge in Switching and routing, IP fundamentals, Subnetting.
  • CCNA course is additional advantage.
  • Good knowledge in SDH/DWDM equipment maintenance and troubleshooting.
  • Good knowledge and understanding of the fiber network, optical power budget
  • At least 1-2 Year in NOC related operation mainly in MPLS-TP and SDH transport field.
  • Basic understanding of Linux.
